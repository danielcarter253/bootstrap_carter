What is Bad Time Games?

BTG is a portfolio website of sorts. Everything you see or read here is created by myself, Daniel Carter, with the images being provided from the press kits released for each game. Each press kit can be found at igdb.com. None of this is for profit, just a showcase of the work I've done in the past couple years. Most of these articles can be found at JMU's The Breeze and if you click the pretty link here on this page it'll take you to the video game section of the website. I hope you enjoy the content and game on my friends.
